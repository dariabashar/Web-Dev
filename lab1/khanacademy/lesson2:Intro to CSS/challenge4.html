beautiful zoo!

A zoo is an exciting place to connect with the animal world! Here, you can see rare and exotic creatures, learn fascinating facts about nature, and enjoy quality time with your family surrounded by amazing wildlife.

What can I see here?

Here you can meet majestic lions and tigers, graceful giraffes and zebras, friendly pandas and playful monkeys, as well as exotic parrots and bright flamingos.

What can't you do at the zoo?

  1. Do not feed the animal — it can harm their health.
  2. Do not touch the animals — it can be dangerous for both you and them.
  3. Do not make loud noises or tap on enclosure glass — it stresses the animals.
  4. Do not cross the barriers — this is strictly prohibited for your safety.

How to get to us?

Our zoo is conveniently located in the city center, making it easily accessible for everyone. You can reach us by public transport: bus and tram stops are just a 5-minute walk away. If you are arriving by car, there is a spacious parking lot near the zoo. Address: 25 Central Street, next to the city park.

Don't forget to:

  1. Bring your camera to capture memorable moments.
  2. Wear comfortable shoes for walking around.
  3. Check the feeding schedule for your favorite animals.
