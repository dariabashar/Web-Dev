Pasta Alfredo!

Pasta Alfredo recipe

  • Time:30 mins
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
24 ounces dry fettuccine pasta
1 cup butter
¾ pint heavy cream
salt and pepper to taste
1 dash garlic salt
¾ cup grated Romano cheeset
½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Step 1: Gather all ingredients.

Step 2:Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Add fettuccine and cook for 8 to 10 minutes or until al dente; drain.

Step 3: Melt butter into cream in a large saucepan over low heat; add salt, pepper, and garlic salt.

Step 4: Increase the heat to medium; stir in grated Romano and Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce has thickened.

Step 5: Add cooked pasta to sauce and toss until thoroughly coated; serve immediately.
